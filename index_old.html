Discussion

Introduction

This visualization is created from dataset sf_crime.geojson and the idea is to find any relevant information that can be extracted from it. In this visualization you can see the areas with most concentrated crime rates in the city of San Francisco.

Methods

This visualization takes the aggregation of the marked crime geographic locations and shows the areas with most crime in yeloow color, but the areas with the least crime in dark blue as seen in the legend. The dataset consists of 10000 different crime locations and types at different times in San Francico, but here they are all plotted at the same time on the map in order to find out, which is the area that has the most crime.

Results

As a result, we can see which exact points are the most dangerous in the city and have the most crime overall. Almost all of the highest points are located side by side in the city.
